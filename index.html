📜 Platform Rules & Terms

Please read before continuing

1. Points, Not Cash

Points used in ABI Bingo have no fixed cash value. Balances are assigned and adjusted manually by admins after verification and chat approval.

2. Deposits & Withdrawals

Any point deposit or withdrawal request must go through admin/CEO chat approval. Automated payments are not part of this platform.

3. Fair Play

Cards are randomly assigned. Manually editing your card, balance, or game results outside the app is prohibited and may result in a permanent ban.

4. Account Verification

Access to betting requires email verification and admin approval. Admins may request an ID photo via chat for account verification.

5. Conduct

Abusive behavior toward admins or other players in chat is not tolerated and may result in suspension.

6. Changes

Admins may update points, balances, or access status at any time as part of platform management.
ABI BINGO

How to play

1
Browse the lobby — see live games, online players, and room info in real time.
2
Get approved — before your first bet, tap the chat icon and send a message and photo to an admin for verification.
3
Get points — once approved an admin assigns your point balance. Each game costs 30 points.
4
Join during betting phase — before numbers start calling, a random winning pattern is announced and you get a random unique card.
5
Play — B(1-15) I(16-30) N(31-45) G(46-60) O(61-75). Mark called numbers on your card to match the announced pattern.
6
Win automatically — the instant your card completes the announced pattern, your win is verified and the round stops!
