How to play

1
Create your account — verify your email, then browse the app freely.
2
Get approved — before your first bet, tap the chat icon and send a message and photo to an admin for verification.
3
Get points — once approved an admin assigns your point balance. Each game costs 30 points.
4
Join during betting phase — 30 second window before numbers start calling.
5
Play — B(1-15) I(16-30) N(31-45) G(46-60) O(61-75). Mark numbers and press BINGO when you complete a line!
6
Win types — 1 LINE (25pts) · 2 LINE (50pts) · FULL BINGO (100pts).
